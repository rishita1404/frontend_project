Advanced German Vocabulary Learning

Erleuchtung

Definition: Enlightenment or illumination. Often used in philosophical or spiritual contexts.

Example: Die Erleuchtung kam nach Jahren der Meditation. (Enlightenment came after years of meditation.)

Zerstreuung

Definition: Distraction or diversion. It refers to a state of being distracted or to activities that divert attention.

Example: Die Zerstreuung während der Besprechung war störend. (The distraction during the meeting was disturbing.)

Verschwendung

Definition: Waste or extravagance. Refers to the unnecessary use of resources.

Example: Die Verschwendung von Lebensmitteln ist in vielen Teilen der Welt ein großes Problem. (The waste of food is a big problem in many parts of the world.)

Unabhängigkeit

Definition: Independence. Often used in political contexts, but also refers to personal autonomy.

Example: Die Unabhängigkeit der Nation wurde nach vielen Jahren des Kampfes erreicht. (The independence of the nation was achieved after many years of struggle.)

Schadenfreude

Definition: The pleasure derived from another person's misfortune. A uniquely German term that describes this feeling.

Example: Er konnte seine Schadenfreude nicht verbergen, als sein Rivale scheiterte. (He couldn’t hide his schadenfreude when his rival failed.)
